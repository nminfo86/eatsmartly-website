#### 1. **Hero Section - Like AnyDesk**
   - ✅ Changed from plain white to gradient background (#0b7a61 → #0a5c4a)
   - ✅ White text on dark background (better contrast)
   - ✅ Added decorative circular element for depth
   - ✅ Added prominent screenshot showcase area
   - ✅ Improved button styles with shadows and hover effects

   - ✅ Larger padding (15px 40px) for better touch targets
   - ✅ Added box-shadow for depth

   - ✅ Better image centering and spacing
